Executive Dashboard

Finding

Revenue reached 2,326,534 with a profit of 292,297 during the 2023–2027 period. Although both showed a growth trend, revenue and profit fluctuated almost every month. In addition, the West Region recorded the highest sales (739,814), and the Technology Category was the largest contributor to sales (839,893).

Insight

The company successfully increased revenue and profit during the analysis period; however, monthly fluctuations indicate that sales growth has not been consistent. On the other hand, the largest contributions came from the West Region and the Technology Category, suggesting that current business growth is still driven more by specific regions and categories rather than an even distribution of performance across the entire business.

Recommendation

Conduct a further analysis of the periods with the highest and lowest sales performance to identify factors influencing the fluctuations, such as seasonal patterns, promotional effectiveness, or changes in customer demand. Additionally, evaluate the strategies successfully implemented in the West Region and the Technology Category to determine whether these approaches can be adapted to regions and categories with lower performance, thereby making sales growth more even and sustainable.

Customer Dashboard

Findings

The Consumer segment generated total sales of 1,170,660 and accounted for 51.74% of total customers, making it the segment with the largest contribution to sales and the largest number of customers. Additionally, Sean Miller is the customer with the highest total purchases, at 25,043.

Insight

The Consumer segment is the company’s primary source of revenue because it has the highest number of customers and total sales. This indicates that the majority of the company’s revenue comes from individual customers, so the performance of this segment significantly impacts the company’s total sales. Furthermore, the presence of several customers with transaction values significantly higher than those of other customers suggests that the company has high-value customers who can potentially be retained through loyalty strategies.

Recommendation

Maintain performance in the Consumer Segment through loyalty programs and promotions to sustain its revenue contribution. Conduct further analysis of the Corporate and Home Office Segments to identify the factors causing lower sales contributions. Evaluate whether marketing strategies, flagship products, or purchasing patterns in the Consumer Segment can be adapted to the Corporate and Home Office segments. Develop specific strategies for high-value customers, such as loyalty programs or exclusive offers, to improve customer retention.

Regional Dashboard

Finding

California recorded the highest sales of 457,688 and the highest profit of 76,381 compared to other states. Regionally, the West was the region with the largest contribution to sales and profit, while some states still showed low or even negative profits.

Insight

California’s dominance in sales and profit indicates that the company’s current business performance remains heavily dependent on certain regions, particularly the West. This situation presents growth opportunities through improved performance in other states and regions where contributions are still lower.

Recommendation

Analyze California’s success factors, such as product mix, customer characteristics, and marketing strategies implemented. Identify states with high sales but low or negative profit to determine whether there are issues with cost structure, discounts, or operational efficiency. Evaluate whether strategies that have been successful in California can be adapted to other states with similar market characteristics.

Product Dashboard

Findings
The Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier was the top-selling product with sales of 61,600. In contrast, the Cubify CubeX 3D Printer Deluxe recorded the largest loss, with a profit of -8,880. At the subcategory level, Copiers generated the highest profit of 56,094. Additionally, the Discount vs. Profit visualization shows a trend where products with higher discount rates have lower profits, and some even incur losses.


Insight

High-selling products do not always contribute the most profit, so the company needs to evaluate product performance based on profitability, not just sales volume. On the other hand, the Copiers subcategory demonstrates a strong ability to generate high profit, while the relationship between discount and profit indicates that offering excessive discounts has the potential to reduce the company’s profits.

Recommendation

Evaluate the discount strategy, particularly for products that continue to generate low profit despite high sales. Conduct an analysis of the products with the largest losses, such as the Cubify CubeX 3D Printer Deluxe, to determine whether the cause stems from excessive discounts, operating costs, or low market demand.
